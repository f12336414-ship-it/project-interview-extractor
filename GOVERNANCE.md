# Governance

Project Interview Extractor currently uses a maintainer-led governance model.

## Maintainer

The current maintainer is [@f12336414-ship-it](https://github.com/f12336414-ship-it).

The maintainer is responsible for roadmap decisions, releases, security response, repository settings, and merging contributions.

## Decision making

- Small and reversible decisions are made through pull-request review.
- Material changes to truthfulness rules, evidence levels, scoring, licensing, privacy, or compatibility should begin with a public issue or proposal.
- Decisions should cite observable user needs, evaluation cases, or maintenance costs.
- Security-sensitive discussion may remain private until disclosure is safe.

## Becoming a maintainer

Regular contributors may be invited after demonstrating sustained, constructive work, sound judgment around privacy and evidence, and reliable review participation. Maintainer changes will be documented publicly.

## Project continuity

If the maintainer becomes unavailable, trusted contributors may propose a stewardship transfer or a maintained fork under the Apache-2.0 license.
